Algorithm

  • Based upon Maximum Likelihood Estimate (MLE)
  • User enters one word - choose MLE word from trOznVety, trRozVety, trTazVety 2-grams from first two words
  • User enters two words - similar to one word
  • User enters three words - choose MLE word from trOznVety, trRozVety, trTazVety 4-grams

  • User enters more words

    • Take first two words and predict type of sentence
    • Use appropriate 4-grams from sentence types trOznVety, trRozVety, trTazVety for prediction
